## My Methods

Although I came to prominance via my "Doktor Snake's Voodoo Spellbook", the reality is, I always used psychotronic methods in my workings.   
Psychotronics formed the key.   
The very bedrock.

And I always used location — places of power — to conduct my workings.

The formative realm where you can harness power and get things done.

## Mounds
Where I live in Suffolk there are many Bronze Age and Neolithic mounds.   
Mysterious places, where the atmosphere feels almost inhuman — in the sense that if you go back far enough, these places of power were recognised and used by Neanderthals.

Over near the village of Croxton, if you wander out into the woods, you get a strong feeling of being alone and you can sense the unyielding, monumental power of nature itself.   
And on many occasions I have seen UFOs and cryptids in and around that area.
