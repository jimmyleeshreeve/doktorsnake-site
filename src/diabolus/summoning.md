I carry out the summoning in the wilds of Suffolk, at midnight — often near a ruined church by the sea, where the wind howls and the atmosphere is ripe for spirit contact.

The working involves:

- Inscribing the demon’s sigil on a clay tablet
- Intoning ancient infernal incantations
- Petitioning the spirit with focus and will
- Burying the tablet to seal the rite

This isn’t armchair occultism. It’s direct action. I do the dirty work so you don’t have to.
