# Archangel Ritual

<p class="text">Spiritual cleansing and protection from malevolent forces.</p>

<img class="about-hero" src="/assets/img/archangel-ritual.webp"
     alt="Archangel Ritual">

This working harnesses the spiritual power the archangels to deal with your case. If your situation is difficult, it will prove the catalyst required to keep you protected from malign forces.

Depending on the nature of your situation, I typically call upon one of the four most powerful archangels, which are:

- **MICHAEL ::** Considered the foremost archangel and is depicted as a warrior and protector. His name means "Who is like God" and he is associated with courage, strength, and righteousness. Michael is believed to have defeated Lucifer and cast him out of Heaven. He is often depicted wielding a sword or holding scales to represent justice.

- **GABRIEL ::** His name means "God is my strength" or "Hero of God." Gabriel is known as a messenger and is associated with communication, revelation, and prophecy. In the Bible, Gabriel appears to Daniel to interpret visions and announces the birth of Jesus to Mary. Gabriel is often depicted with a trumpet, symbolizing the heralding of important messages.

- **RAPHAEL ::** His name means "God heals" or "God has healed." He is associated with healing, protection, and guidance. Raphael plays a significant role in the Book of Tobit, where he guides Tobit's son Tobias on a journey and helps him overcome various challenges. Raphael is often depicted carrying a staff or a fish, representing his role in healing and guiding.

- **URIEL ::** His name means "God is my light" or "Light of God." He is associated with wisdom, enlightenment, and illumination. While Uriel is not mentioned by name in the canonical Bible, he is recognized in various religious texts and traditions. Uriel is believed to be an angelic guide who helps humanity understand divine knowledge and offers protection. He is associated with the element of fire.

Where appropriate, I also call upon other archangels, such as Raguel, Sariel, Remiel, and more. However, the above four archangels - Michael, Gabriel, Raphael, and Uriel - are the most widely recognized archangels in Western spiritual contexts, and will help you with the majority of your needs.

**RECOGNIZE: I use the power of the archangels solely for good and for the betterment of those who feel powerless in the world.**

## How It Works

I conduct your ritual at an old disused church (in Eastern England). Typically this is done before noon so the positive, live-affirming energies are at their height.

Once at my chosen place of worship, I go into visionary trance, focusing on your intentions with an opening mantra.

I then call upon the archangel by its name, expressing the pressing need for the archangel's assistance. In my own words, I call upon the archangel to "help our dear friend in need."

At this point, the ceremony becomes one of evocation. I typically see a glowing, golden mist, from out of the super-spectrum. This is a clear sign that your request shall become manifest.

After that I recite a closing mantra, stating that I trust in the archangel's intercession according to universal dynamics, and that I give humble thanks.
