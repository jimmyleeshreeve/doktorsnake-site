# The Three Pillars Working

## Right Work · Sacred Respect · Enduring Wealth

This bespoke working has been created specifically for your circumstances. It combines a symbolic field working, personalised field notes, practical guidance and reflective exercises into a single programme designed to help you move forward with greater confidence, clarity and purpose.

### Your Three Areas of Focus

- Finding a fulfilling part-time Telehealth or counselling role.
- Creating greater harmony, understanding and respect within your home.
- Building long-term financial security through thoughtful decision-making.

The Three Pillars represent the areas of life requiring the greatest attention at this stage of your journey.

### The Key

The opening of new opportunities.

Focused on recognising suitable work, acting with confidence, and moving towards employment that fits your skills, values and preferred way of living.

### The Lamp

Light within the household.

Supporting clearer communication, greater understanding, healthier boundaries and a calmer atmosphere between those who share your life.

### The Stone Treasury

Building lasting security.

Encouraging thoughtful financial decisions, long-term security and the confidence to recognise genuine opportunities while avoiding unnecessary risk.

### The Serpent of Wisdom

Clear sight and discernment.

A reminder to pause before acting, recognise manipulation, question easy answers and make decisions from a state of clarity.

## Your Working Includes

- The symbolic field working is conducted at the twin Bronze Age mounds at SnakeWood in Suffolk, a landscape that has become central to many of my field operations.
- A personalised sigil designed around your intentions, serving as a visual point of focus that you can keep on your phone or revisit whenever you wish to reconnect with the working.
- A written Field Report documenting the operation.
- Private audio discussion exploring the psychology of crypto investing, common mistakes, practical lessons and long-term thinking. This is not financial advice or investment recommendations, but a discussion intended to help you approach decisions with greater confidence and discernment.
- A practical career activation exercise for applications and interviews.
- A relationship communication and boundary framework tailored to your situation.
- Follow-up recommendations as the situation develops.
